    \section*{Koine Greek Demonstrative Pronouns -- \grc{ὅδε}}

    ``\textbf{333.} Demonstrative Pronouns. --The chief demonstrative pronouns are \grc{ὅδε} \fontspec{} this (here), \grc{οὗτος} \fontspec{} this, that, \grc{ἐκεῖνος} \fontspec{} that (there, yonder).''
    \linkfoot{Demonstrative Pronouns, Smyth §333}{https://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/text?doc=Perseus\%3Atext\%3A1999.04.0007\%3Apart\%3D2\%3Achapter\%3D15}{2025-05-23}

    ``Demonstrative pronouns `demonstrate' something; using them is the verbal equivalent of pointing at something or someone. They draw attention to the thing or person you’re referring to.''
    \linkfoot{Demonstrative Pronouns}{https://www.scribbr.com/nouns-and-pronouns/demonstrative-pronouns/}{2025-05-23}
