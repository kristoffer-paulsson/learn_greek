    \linkfoot{Interrogative, Smyth §334}{https://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/text?doc=Perseus\%3Atext\%3A1999.04.0007\%3Apart\%3D2\%3Achapter\%3D15}{2025-05-24}
